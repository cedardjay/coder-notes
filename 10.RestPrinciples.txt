nested ressources

Use /loan-requests/{id}/transactions because:

    ✅ Follows REST conventions

    ✅ Clearly shows the relationship

    ✅ Easier to understand for other developers

    ✅ Matches industry standards

    ✅ Allows future nested resources like /loan-requests/{id}/repayment-schedule

The only time to use /transactions/loan-request/{id} is if loan-request is actually a property of Transaction that you're filtering by — but even then, query parameters are cleaner: /transactions?loanRequestId={id}.
